Chicken Ramen

Description

This recipe for chicken ramen is a comforting and hearty meal that's perfect for chilly days. The broth is made with chicken and a blend of savory spices, and the noodles are cooked until tender. Serve with soft-boiled eggs, sliced chicken, and plenty of scallions for a satisfying bowl of ramen.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon mirin
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 4 packages ramen noodles
  • 4 soft-boiled eggs
  • 4 scallions, sliced

Steps

  1. In a large pot, bring the chicken broth, water, garlic, ginger, soy sauce, mirin, and sesame oil to a boil.
  2. Add the chicken breasts to the pot and let simmer for 20 minutes or until cooked through.
  3. Remove the chicken breasts from the broth and shred with two forks.
  4. Cook the ramen noodles according to package instructions and divide among 4 bowls.
  5. Divide the chicken and broth among the bowls of noodles.
  6. Top each bowl with a soft-boiled egg and sliced scallions.
