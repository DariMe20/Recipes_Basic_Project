Taiyaki

Description

Taiyaki is a popular Japanese snack that's shaped like a fish and filled with sweet red bean paste. The batter is similar to pancake batter and is cooked in a fish-shaped mold. Taiyaki can be enjoyed hot or cold, and is perfect for a sweet treat any time of day.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup sweet red bean paste

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Add the egg and milk to the bowl and whisk until smooth.
  3. Heat a taiyaki pan over medium heat and grease with cooking spray.
  4. Pour the batter into the mold until it's about 2/3 full.
  5. Add a tablespoon of sweet red bean paste to the center of the batter.
  6. Cover the red bean paste with more batter until the mold is full.
  7. Cook the taiyaki for 2-3 minutes on each side or until golden brown.
  8. Remove from the pan and let cool before serving.
